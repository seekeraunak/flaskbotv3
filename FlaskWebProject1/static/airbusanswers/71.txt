P3 was an interim software standard that came in for our Long Range (LR) Airbus Flight Management Systems (FMS) ie. it came in after Step 1 and before Step 1A. It was basically a software standard that included several fixes to Airbus Operational Engineering Bulletins (OEBs). FMGEC P/N C12858CA02 could be at Step 1 or P3 standard depending on the Flight Management (FM) software installed. Software part number PS4087700-903 is P3.
