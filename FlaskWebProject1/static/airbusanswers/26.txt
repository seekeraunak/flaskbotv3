Flight Deck Effects with a Single Reset : - No data is lost on either side. - FM PAGE NOT AVAIL displayed on side that has reset. -Message FMS RESYNCH IN PROGRESS is displayed on both MFDs for approx 6 seconds. - Both FMCs fully operational again after approx 10 seconds. - Automatic FM mode will be lost (NAV auto, CLB auto, SPD auto) if reset is in Master FMC. - Potentially, FMS/GPS POS DISAGREE (EICAS) & FMS1/FMS2 POS DISAGREE (MFD) messages may occur due to Position BIAS issue. - FM Roll command also reset to zero while reset is occurring.
