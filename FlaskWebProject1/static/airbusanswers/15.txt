If at anytime during the cross-loading of a Nav Database, it is suspected the cross-load failed, It is essential you follow the following procedure: -Clear the ARM prompt at 5L on the CDU Maintenance page prior to powering down the units. This insures the transmitting AFMC remains useable. The receiving AFMC will have an incomplete load and have to be reloaded via an ARINC 615 data loader. This will eliminate the need to send both AFMC's to the shop for repair.
